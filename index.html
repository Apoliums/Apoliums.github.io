About Hackathons

A hackathon is an event where individuals, often with backgrounds in programming, software development, design, or other related fields, come together to collaboratively work on creative projects or solve specific problems within a set time frame.

During a hackathon, participants form teams and engage in intense, focused work to develop prototypes, solutions, or proofs of concept. These events typically last anywhere from a few hours to a few days. The goal is to encourage collaboration, creativity, and rapid development.
